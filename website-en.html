About Me

Having recently graduated from Waseda University, I'm looking for an opportunity to jumpstart my career. I have a lifestyle philosophy of "work hard, play hard." I also work with a growth-oriented mindset, which means with time and effort, I know I can improve and keep learning. With my skills and experience, I hope to earn the pleasure of working with you.


As a half-hispanic and half-asian person, diversity and identity has always been an important part of my life. Like many people of mixed race, I felt that I was not really a part of the hispanic and asian communities. Even though I have engaged with both communities, ate their food, and spoke their languages (although limited), something just did not click. For a while, I did not know why. Through lots of traveling and pondering, I have come to realize that identity does not come from race alone. It also comes from one's values, experiences, personality, preferences, and image. These qualities, along with the traits I was born with, is what makes me, well, me! Even though I'm only years old, I'm excited to see how my identity will progress over the years.


I will be grateful if I can share my career and identity's growth with you. I will continue to make an effort until then.
